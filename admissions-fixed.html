Eligibility Criteria
Programme-specific eligibility requirements for admission
General Rule: Most programmes require a Bachelor's degree with Honours / 3-year or 4-year Bachelor's degree from a UGC-recognised university. Special programmes have additional criteria.
General Eligibility (most MA/MSc programmes)
  • A Bachelor's degree with Honours / Honours with Research, OR
  • A 3-year / 6-semester Bachelor's degree, OR
  • A 4-year Bachelor's degree

Until a candidate submits their degree certificate and final mark/grade sheet by the given date, admission offered will remain provisional.

Applicable to: All MA Social Work specialisations, MA Development Studies, MA Women's Studies, MA Environment & Climate Change, MA Regulatory/Water/Urban Policy, MA Media & Cultural Studies, MA Education, MA Social Entrepreneurship, MA Labour Studies, MA/MSc Analytics, MA Livelihoods, MA Cities/Natural Resources/Public Policy & Governance, MA Ecology & Sustainable Development, MA Sociology & Social Anthropology, MA Peace & Conflict Studies, MLISc, MA/MSc Disaster Management, MA/MSc Disaster & Climate Risk Assessment.

Public Health Programmes (MPH)
  • Successfully completed a 3-year Bachelor's Degree in any discipline, OR
  • Bachelor's Degree in medicine, paramedical sciences, pharmacy, veterinary sciences, engineering, or law (4-year programmes), OR
  • Candidates who have successfully completed all requirements of their final year degree (including all examinations and internships)

Applicable to: MPH Health Administration, MPH Social Epidemiology, MPH Health Policy Economics & Finance (all Mumbai Campus)

Applied Psychology (Clinical & Counselling Practice)
  • Full-time B.A./B.Sc. Honours in Psychology (regular mode), OR
  • Full-time B.A./B.Sc. General with major/minor in Psychology or up to 6 credits in psychology (regular mode), OR
  • Any Bachelor's Degree + University-recognised 1-year PG Diploma in Counselling (minimum 40 credits) - Distance/online diploma NOT accepted
Law Programmes (LL.M / Integrated MA+LLM)
  • 3-Year LL.B. after Graduation (10+2+3) OR 5-year Integrated LL.B. (10+2+5) recognised by Bar Council of India
  • Minimum 50% marks in LL.B. aggregate (45% for SC/ST)

Applicable to: LL.M in Access to Justice (Mumbai), Integrated MA Public Policy & Governance + LLM (Hyderabad-Mumbai)

B.Ed - M.Ed (3-Year Integrated)
  • Undergraduate/PG degree in any academic discipline (Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, Medicine, Pharmacy, etc.) from UGC-recognised university
  • Admission to relevant Pedagogic Specialisation based on disciplinary major
  • Candidates with vocational/professional degrees advised to contact TISS prior to applying

Programme is 3 years full-time with exit option after 2 years with B.Ed degree.

M.Ed (2-Year Lateral Entry)
  • UG/PG degree in academic discipline (Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, Medicine, Pharmacy, etc.) from UGC-recognised university, AND
  • B.Ed / BEIEd / BABEd / BscBEd / ITEP degree from UGC-recognised university

2-year full-time programme by Lateral Entry into B.Ed-M.Ed Programme.

Selection Criteria & Admission Rules
How the merit list is prepared and what rules apply
Admission to all PG programmes is based on CUET-PG 2026 score (weightage 100%).
There will be NO Extempore Reflection Test / Group Discussions / Online Personal Interviews.

Merit List Preparation

  • Open Merit List (OML): Prepared based on CUET-PG score without considering reservation category
  • Category-wise Merit List (CML): Prepared after OML for reserved seats as per GoI/UGC norms
  • Tie-breaking: In case of tie, higher CGPA of Bachelor's Degree is used
  • Max programmes: Candidates can apply to maximum THREE programmes
  • Candidates admitted to only ONE programme (where fee is paid)

Reservation Policy (Mumbai/Tuljapur/Hyderabad)

  • SC: 15% of total seats
  • ST: 7.5% of total seats
  • OBC (Non-Creamy Layer): 27% of total seats
  • EWS: 10% of total seats
  • PWD (Supernumerary): 5% of seats
  • Armed Forces (Supernumerary): 5% of seats
  • Kashmiri Migrants (KM): Supernumerary seats with 10% cut-off relaxation

Guwahati Campus (Special Policy)

  • 66% of seats reserved for candidates from 8 North-Eastern states
  • Specific quota allotted for each of the 8 states
  • Seat allotment governed by reservation policy of respective state
  • 34% of seats for candidates from rest of India (GoI reservation policy applies)

Deputed & International Candidates

  • Officers deputed by Central/State Governments may be admitted via Written Test / Online Personal Interview
  • CUET score NOT mandatory for deputed/sponsored candidates
  • Seats for deputed candidates treated as supernumerary
  • International candidates apply through TISS Office for International Affairs (OIA)
  • All foreign nationals eligible; seats are supernumerary
Key Admission Rules
Document Verification Rules
  • All selected candidates must report to campus for document verification as per admission timeline
  • Failure to report forfeits admission - no correspondence will be entertained
  • Original mark sheets/grade sheets/certificates must be produced
  • Only attested mark sheets downloaded from the internet are accepted
  • Migration Certificates must be submitted by 31st October 2026
  • Currently employed candidates must submit leave sanction/relieving order
  • Medical check-up by TISS Medical Officer is compulsory
Cancellation of Admission
  • Student does not fulfil eligibility requirements
  • Student used fraudulent means to secure admission
  • Student made false/incorrect information in application
  • Discrepancy found between application details and documents

Candidates must join the programme within 7 days from programme commencement or 7 days of fee payment, whichever is later.

Application Fee Payment
  • Payment via Credit Card / Debit Card / NEFT / UPI / Net Banking only
  • Demand drafts and pay orders are NOT accepted
  • Application fee once paid is NON-REFUNDABLE
  • Filling more than one application form leads to cancellation of all applications

How to Apply
Step-by-step guide to fill the TISS PG application form
Applications are submitted online at admissions.tiss.ac.in. You need your CUET-PG 2026 registration details.
1

Register for CUET-PG 2026

Visit nta.ac.in and register for CUET-PG 2026. Select the relevant test paper code(s) for the TISS programme(s) you wish to apply to. CUET score is the sole basis for admission (100% weightage).

2

Appear for CUET-PG Examination

Sit for the CUET-PG 2026 examination as per NTA's schedule and your chosen test paper code(s).

3

Visit TISS Admissions Portal

Go to admissions.tiss.ac.in when the TISS application window opens. Read all programme descriptions and eligibility criteria carefully before choosing programmes.

4

Fill the Online Application Form

Select up to THREE programmes in ONE application form. Do NOT fill more than one form - all applications will be cancelled if you do. Provide the same mobile number and email used during CUET-PG 2026 registration.

5

Upload Required Documents

Scan and upload the following documents as per your category:

  • All candidates: Recent photograph + Signature
  • EWS: Income & Asset Certificate for FY 2025-26 (annual family income <Rs 8 lakh)
  • SC/ST: Caste certificate + Income Certificate for FY 2025-26
  • OBC(NC): OBC Non-Creamy Layer certificate issued after 01-04-2026 (must be in Central Govt format) + Income Certificate
  • PWD: Disability certificate (...40% disability) + UDID Card
  • KM: Kashmiri Migrant certificate from Relief/Rehabilitation Commissioner
  • Armed Forces: Certificate issued by Zila/Rajya/Kendriya Sainik Board
6

Pay the Application Fee

Pay online via Credit Card / Debit Card / NEFT / UPI / Net Banking. Application fee is non-refundable. Confirm all data before payment - changes are not allowed after submission.

7

Check Admission Portal Periodically

Monitor the TISS admissions portal for merit list publication, waitlist updates, and further instructions. Merit list, waitlist, and all selection statuses are displayed on the portal.

8

Pay Admission Fee & Confirm Seat

If selected, pay the programme fee as per the admission portal instructions to confirm your seat. You will be admitted to only ONE programme.

9

Report to Campus for Document Verification

Report to your respective TISS campus on the stipulated date with original documents. Undergo medical check-up by TISS Medical Officer. Submit Migration Certificate (or undertaking to submit by 31 Oct 2026).

Scholarships & Financial Aid
GoI Post-Matric Scholarship (GoI-PMS)
  • SC/ST candidates with parental/guardian income <Rs 2.5 lakhs/year are eligible
  • Pay basic fees initially; scholarship from State Government credited via DBT
  • OBC(NC) candidates with parental income <Rs 1 lakh/year: processed by SC/ST & EO Cell
  • Scholarship applications must be filled ON TIME as per State Government procedures
  • Failure to fill GoI-PMS form means full programme fees must be paid
Educational Loans
  • TISS has arrangements with nationalized banks for educational loans
  • Office of Student Affairs coordinates the process
  • Contact TISS Student Affairs office for support
  • Obtaining loans is the sole responsibility of the candidate
  • Admissions Helpline: 022-25525252
Fee Exemption for Employees
  • Serving permanent TISS employees, their spouses, and up to two children are exempt from tuition fees
  • Exemption NOT applicable for self-financing programmes
Required Documents for GoI-PMS
  • Link Saving Bank Account with Aadhaar Card (Aadhaar-Enabled Account / AEA)
  • Link Aadhaar to mobile number
  • After joining: Submit AEA Passbook copy, Mobile No., Email ID, self-attested Aadhaar copy to SC/ST & EO Cell
